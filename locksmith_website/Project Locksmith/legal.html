01 — Privacy

Privacy Policy

Acme Services is a mobile locksmith serving Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area. We respect your privacy and handle your personal information in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA). This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

When you call us, fill out a form, or send us a message, we may collect:

  • Your name and phone number, so we can reach you about your job;
  • The postal code or address where you need service, so we can dispatch a technician to the right place;
  • The details of your request — the problem, the type of property or vehicle, and any notes you add;
  • Any photos you choose to send to help us understand the job.

We ask only for what we need to quote, dispatch, and complete the work. You don't need to create an account to contact us.

How we use it

We use your information to respond to your request, confirm an estimate, dispatch a technician to your location, contact you about the job (including text/SMS updates such as "technician en route"), and keep a record of the work for our guarantee and for accounting.

Your consent

By contacting us or submitting a request, you consent to us collecting and using your information for the purposes above. You can withdraw your consent at any time — see Your rights below.

✓ We never sell your information. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, and we don't use it for third-party advertising.

Who we share it with

We share your information only with the technician assigned to your job and with the trusted service providers that help us operate — for example, secure hosting and messaging providers — who are required to protect it and use it only to provide those services. We may disclose information if required by law, or where necessary to establish or defend our legal rights.

Photos are deleted automatically

Photos you send through our contact form or that are attached to a job are automatically deleted from our system 24 hours after they're uploaded. We keep them only long enough to handle your request.

How we protect it

Your information is stored on secured systems, and access to our dispatch and customer records is password-protected and limited to authorized staff. While no method of transmission or storage is ever completely secure, we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information.

How long we keep it

We keep job records for as long as we need them to provide the service, honour our work guarantee, and meet our legal and accounting obligations. After that, we securely dispose of them.

Cookies & this website

Our website stores small amounts of data in your browser (local storage) so it works properly — for example, your language preference and cached page content. We do not run third-party advertising or cross-site tracking. Our service-area map is loaded from a third-party map provider.

Your rights

Under PIPEDA, you may ask us to access the personal information we hold about you, correct it, withdraw your consent, or delete it. Email us at our contact address and we'll respond within a reasonable time, generally within 30 days. If you have a concern we can't resolve, you may contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version.

03 — Warranty

Work Guarantee

Every job is backed by our satisfaction guarantee. We stand behind our workmanship — if something we installed or serviced doesn't work the way it should because of our work, we'll come back and make it right.

  • Workmanship guaranteed. If a lock we rekeyed, repaired, or installed fails because of our installation or labour, we'll return and correct it at no additional labour cost.
  • Parts & hardware. Locks and hardware we supply are covered by the manufacturer's warranty, and we'll help you make a claim if you ever need to.
  • Non-destructive entry. Wherever possible we open locks without damage. If drilling or replacement is the only option, we'll tell you first and get your okay before proceeding.

What isn't covered

  • Pre-existing damage, rust, or normal wear and tear;
  • Hardware you supplied yourself;
  • Misuse, or forced entry or tampering by someone else after we've finished.

How to make a claim

Call us at — or email — with your job details, and we'll arrange a visit. This guarantee is in addition to any rights you have under applicable consumer-protection law.
