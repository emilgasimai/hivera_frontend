Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Draft. This is a plain-language draft, not legal advice. Have it reviewed by a lawyer before the site collects real account data (email/password), particularly under Ontario/Canada privacy law (PIPEDA).

This policy explains what information Hivera collects through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep it short and plain on purpose.

What we collect

We only collect what you choose to send us:

  • Project enquiries. When you use the “Start a Project” form, we collect your name, email address, business type, and the project description you write, plus any optional details you add (such as an estimated budget).
  • Accounts (once available). Hivera visitor accounts are not live yet. When they are, creating one will collect your email address and a password (stored only as a securely hashed value, never in plain text), along with any preferences you save, such as favorited projects.

How we use it

We use this information only to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and discuss a possible project.
  • Provide and manage your account, if you create one.

We do not use your information for anything unrelated to the reason you gave it to us.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We don’t share it for advertising. Information is only handled by the service providers we rely on to run the site and store data (for example, our hosting and database providers), and only so those services can function.

Cookies & analytics

This site currently uses no tracking cookies and no analytics. If that changes — for example, if we add privacy-respecting analytics — we will update this policy to say exactly what is used and why.

Data retention & deletion

We keep enquiry details only as long as needed to follow up with you. You can ask us to delete your information — including an account, once accounts exist — at any time. Email hello@hivera.app and we’ll remove it.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email hello@hivera.app.
